Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 26, 2025

We respect your privacy. This policy describes how Mint Box ("We") handles information when you use our Services at https://www.mintbox.space/ and the Telegram Mini App.

1. Information We Collect

  • TON Wallet Address and connection status via TON Connect (provided by your wallet).
  • Content you upload (images and metadata) for storage on IPFS/Pinata.
  • Basic technical information (error logs, interaction events) for operation and service improvement purposes.

2. How We Use Information

  • To perform IPFS upload functions and send NFT minting requests to your wallet.
  • To diagnose errors, improve performance, and enhance user experience.

3. Storage and Sharing

  • Your metadata and files are stored on IPFS/Pinata infrastructure (third-party service).
  • We do not sell your personal data. Information may be shared when necessary to comply with the law or protect legal rights.

4. Security

We apply reasonable measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission/storage on the Internet is absolutely secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • You can disconnect your wallet in the TON wallet app at any time.
  • You control the content uploaded and can stop using the Service at any time.

6. Sensitive Data

We do not request or store seed phrases, private keys, or traditional payment information. All transactions are performed directly within your wallet.

7. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated. Changes are effective when posted on this page.

8. Contact

Please contact us via Telegram if you have any questions regarding privacy.
